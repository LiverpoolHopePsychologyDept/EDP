How will this help me for my assessment?

These tasks demonstrate how to uses conds files to present stimuli, apply a correct response to the stimulus presentation, and state which level of the IV is represented.

You will need to understand IVs, levels (conditions) and DVs for the method section. You will also need to know how to present stimuli for the psychopy part of the assessment.
