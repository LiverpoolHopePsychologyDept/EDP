How will this help me for my assessment?

As you know, you must submit a method section in the format of a journal article as a part of the assessment. By reading and critiquing the method sections above you should be in a strong position to appreciate what makes a method section good

Remember one of the key criteria for a good grade it to ensure that anyone could replicate your study with the information that you provide in the method section
