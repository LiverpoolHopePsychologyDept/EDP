penning@hope.ac.uk palumbl@hope.ac.uk
Glen has over 25 years’ experience in psychology and using software to create experiments for research with staff and students. She also has over 15 years’ experience in curriculum design and creating course materials and content. Over the years she has taught various software packages to students, including (but not limited to) PsychoPy Builder, E-Prime, jamovi, JASP, SPSS, Excel. Letizia is a cognitive psychologist based in the School of Psychology. Her research delves into the cognitive processes underlying our engagement with art, objects, and space design, considering individual differences. Letizia has a deep knowledge of PsychoPy and programming experiments for psychological research.
